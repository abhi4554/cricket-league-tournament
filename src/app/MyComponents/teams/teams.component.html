Strengths and weakness Of Team India

If referred to stats alone that Team India’s top 3 is the best top 3 in the world currently i.e Rohit Sharma,Shikhar Dhawan and Virat Kohli and it is Team India’s best strength going into the World Cup 2019.Considering Wrist spin would play a vital role in the World Cup, the role of these two would be very important if India have to win the tournament.
Looking at the middle-order as a whole, it looks slightly shallow. Also, based on historical performances, in case the top 3 fail together, the confidence that India’s middle-order shall cope up with any challenge is still missing.

Strengths and weakness of Team Australia

The comeback of Steve Smith and David Warner post the one year ban followed by the sandpaper saga is the biggest boost for Australia.
They have only won four matches and lost 19 in the last two years time.This is the worst stats for any team in the cricket world. Aaron Finch’s struggle is a big concern as he is not able to give a solid start

Strengths and weakness of Team New Zealand

Ross Taylor, the most experienced player will be a key for the campaign for the Kiwis. Taylor, along with the skipper, Williamson is the backbone of their batting line up.
The pace attack of this team is really impressive and has been delivering well consistently.

Strength and weakness of Team South Africa

Apart from a world-class top three batsmen in Quinton de Kock, Hashim Amla and Faf du Plessis, South Africa have one of the best bowling attacks at their disposal.
Chris Morris has been selected in the squad as an all-rounder and the lanky cricketer will have to do a lot with the bat apart from bowling some economical spells for the Proteas.
